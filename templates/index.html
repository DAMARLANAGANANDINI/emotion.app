Emotion Recognition

This Web Application is used for recognizing Emotion based on Facial Expressions.Emotion recognition is a fascinating field that focuses on understanding and interpreting human emotions through various signals such as facial expressions and gives result as voice tone,text. Emotion recognition has numerous applications across different industries, including healthcare, education, marketing, and entertainment. By analyzing emotions, we can gain valuable insights into human behavior, improve user experiences, and develop more empathetic and personalized technologies.
